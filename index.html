What is WakianTech?

Pronounced WAH-kee-en-TEK, we are a game, website and software developer studio located in Brunswick Maine. Our goal is to create unique, fun and engaging indie games that break the usual/common mold. We also develop software and websites, which we thrive to make more in the near future.
